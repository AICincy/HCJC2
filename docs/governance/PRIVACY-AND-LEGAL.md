# Privacy and Legal

> **Authority:** Approved controlled HCJC2 governance specification. It defines V2 requirements for this subject and delegates shared terminology to the canonical reference.

## Scope

This document controls the HCJC2 privacy and legal requirements. Shared concepts are defined in the [HCJC Canonical Cross-Version Reference](../reference/HCJC-CANONICAL-REFERENCE.md).

## Controlled rules

## V2-85 Public Records Purpose

HCJC2 structures and explains approved public records while preserving source attribution and limitations.

## V2-86 Presumption of Innocence

Custody, arrest, charge, and correlation presentation states that allegations and public records do not establish guilt.

## V2-87 Consumer Reporting Restriction

The system is not a consumer reporting agency and prohibits employment, tenant, credit, insurance, and comparable eligibility use.

## V2-88 Independent Non-Governmental Status

The interface clearly identifies its independent status and does not imply governmental operation or endorsement.

## V2-89 No-Fee Corrections

Users receive an accessible no-fee process to challenge inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading published information.

## V2-90 Sealing and Removal

Sealing, expungement, removal, and current-photo requests have documented intake, review, disposition, publication, and evidence behavior.

## V2-91 Correlation Disputes

Public inferred relationships provide a stable dispute route and review lifecycle.

## V2-92 Indexing and Social Metadata

Individual custody pages use no-index controls and avoid individualized social-preview cards.

## V2-93 Current Photo Eligibility

Public booking photographs remain available only while eligible under current-roster and approved retention rules.

## V2-94 Public and Private Classification

Every field, artifact, preview, review record, operational log, and evidence item has an explicit publication classification.

## V2-95 New Feed and Third-Party Review

New feeds, analytics, scripts, integrations, and relationship families require privacy, legal, security, and public-purpose review.

## V2-96 Controlled Public Language

The interface distinguishes source-reported facts, normalization, assessments, inferred relationships, review, and unresolved evidence without sensational or guilt-implying language.

## Related decisions, contracts, schemas, tests, and evidence

Related controlled extensions and verification records are linked as they are approved.

## Supporting material

Supporting research, audits, and historical planning remain non-authoritative under `docs/supporting/`.
